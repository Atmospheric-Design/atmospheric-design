Experiments in making

An inseparable part of my interior design work has always been experimenting with different materials and treatments. The process of exploring established techniques, while attempting to remix and reinvent them has led me to the reinvention of cyanotype for wood treatment, and the repurposing of macrame weaving technique in furniture.


Featured project: Atmospheric Wood

Using a historic photographic technique to create a distinct blue coloring on wood surfaces, Atmospheric Wood is a material suitable for a wide variety of residential, hospitality and retail interiors.
