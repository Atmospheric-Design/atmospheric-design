Aion time calendar

Drawing inspiration from Aion’s circle encompassing the universe, the Aion Time is unbound and seamless in its design - like the eternal halo around a deity. Working both as a decorative piece and a functional calendar, you’ll want to keep it for eternity.

Manually adjust the rings by rotating them, matching the opening to one of the 12 months, 31 days, and 7 weekdays.

Treated with the Atmospheric Wood treatment and cut and engraved using computer precision, the Aion time calendar is conceptually a synergy of the organic and the artificial, just as time itself.

The calendar is timeless, as it contains no battery or complicated mechanical parts. It also doesn't interest itself in displaying the year, making it an everlasting set piece for the stylish living room or bedroom.
