Atmospheric oak table

This designer table pairs the natural colour of oak with the blue accent of the Atmospheric Wood treatment. With its rounded shape and exposed inside layer, this luxurious piece of furniture will fit in any midcentury-inspired interior.

The duality of oak and cyanotype create a furniture object that immediately stands out. The exposed storage compartment provides both added utility and enhanced aesthetics to what can easily be the last side table you will ever need.

Designed by Egle Tuleikyte and produced in collaboration with Oak Studio Designs.

Despite it's diminutive size, the table's oak composition and solid construction make the table is capable of holding even a large collection of albums and magazines.
