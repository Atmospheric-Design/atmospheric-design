Geometric coffee table set

Similar, yet different, contrasting yet complementing eachother, these two solid oak tables create a unique pairing that can become the centerpiece of any modern space. These geometry-inspired tables are adorned with tops coloured in blue by the Atmospheric Wood treatment and natural oaken legs.

Part of the original exhibit that showcased Atmospheric Wood at Dutch Design Week 2017, the Geometric coffee table set is still the most popular product of Atmospheric Design.

While designed with a residential living space in mind, this pairing has become part of multiple corporate office, retail and hospitality interiors.
