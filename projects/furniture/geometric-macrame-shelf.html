Geometric macramé shelf

Being purely decorative in its origin, the addition of a functional element to the aesthetics of macramé makes this shelf both a pleasing element of a living space, and a useful storage option.

This geometry-inspired shelf is comprised of wooden shelf and dowel, both coloured in blue by the Atmospheric Wood treatment, and a white woven macramé suspension system.

The rigidity of geometric structures and the organic flow of macrame weaving create a contrasting pairing which is united by the synergetic presence of Atmospheric Wood.

The high load-bearing capacity of the shelf is ensured by the intricate weave of the macrame thread which forms the side suspension ropes.

The central element woven in a geometric pattern serves as a perfect backdrop.
