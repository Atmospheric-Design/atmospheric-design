Atmospheric guitar

Creating a guitar using Atmospheric Wood has been one of the most challenging design projects in my career. The process taught me a lot about the nature of music, the physics of acoustics and the various instrumentation necessary to create a functional and not purely aesthetic object.

From the choice of wood material, to the final polishing touches, the creative process behind the Atmospheric guitar was focused on enhancing the self-expression of the musician.

Working on such a project remains a truly unique experience, and one I am eager to replicate.
