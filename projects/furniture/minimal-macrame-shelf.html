Minimal macramé shelf

Macramé is often used as a decorative wall object without any utility. The addition of a functional element to the aesthetics of macramé makes this shelf both a pleasing element of a living space, and a useful storage option.

This shelf was designed with minimalism in mind, relying solely on a wooden shelf and dowel, both coloured blue by the Atmospheric wood treatment, and a white woven macramé suspension system.
