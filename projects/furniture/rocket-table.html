Rocket table

An intricate table cut with computer precision and coloured by the Atmospheric Wood treatment. Inspired by the design language of NASA and the aesthetic of 90s sci-fi classics such as The Jetsons, the Rocket Table will elevate your living room out of this world!

The Rocket Table is a flexible coffee table with adjustable height. Designed and engineered with toolless assembly in mind, the separate pieces of the table fit into each other and can easily be adjusted based on the owner's preference.

Created from durable plywood and additionally treated with waterproofing solution, the Rocket table can survive even the most demanding atmospheric conditions (and perhaps even space travel).
