Scandinavian dining

A bespoke furniture unit for a residential home in Amsterdam, the Scandinavian dining table and bench set is an example of high quality computer precision in cutting and shaping the wood.

The table seats 8, while the bench structure allows for ample storage capacity underneath.

The unit was designed to fit a new kitchen in the Skandinavian style. The main objective was to maximize the efficient use of space, while future-proofing it for a growing young family.
