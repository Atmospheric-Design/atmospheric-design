Atmospheric spaces

The Atmospheric Wood treatment has a diverse array of usage scenarios and contexts. It can be applied as an accent or as an integral part of an interior. Since it can be applied to almost any wooden surface, the treatment can be used on floors, doors, wall panels, backboards, table tops and other furniture such as tables or chairs.
